the concept

Explore Romania Through Dance began as a dream for Bboy Beat. Four years ago he started teaching breaking workshops all around Romania. 
The project (4 years in the making) inspired us to put 2 and 2 together and create this amazing concept: to tell the stories of romanian artist who represent the Hip Hop culture on a t-shirt. 

behind design

the globe stands for exploring and the various lessons you learn by travelling. The best teacher remains experience. 

the number 4 basically represents the edition of the project, ERTD is in it's 4th year already. The number also represents the journey and the memories.

believing in a community that needs to be heard.
